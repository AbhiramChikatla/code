NS2, or Network Simulator 2, is a discrete-event network simulator used for researching and
simulating computer networks. It's widely used in academia and research to model and
analyze network protocols and performance. Developed in C++ and OTcl (an object-oriented
version of Tcl), NS2 allows users to simulate various networking scenarios, including
different network topologies, routing protocols, and traffic patterns.
